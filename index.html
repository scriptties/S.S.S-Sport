Rules:



#1 do not cross the edge lines or you'll be considered out of the game.
#2 there is only a single runner on each team, he has to dodge the opposing team which is trying to stop him from getting a goal
( each team goes to the side of the runner that isn't on there team ) aswell as 2 defenders.
#3 the defenders have a range around them ( the radius size is decided at the start of the match )
you can't touch the range, and if you go inside you can't leave,
the only way to get a goal would be to make the defender stand near it so its within the range.
